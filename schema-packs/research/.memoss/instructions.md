# Research Vault Instructions

You maintain a research-oriented knowledge base.

- Organize concepts under `topics/` with one concept per page.
- Put external references under `references/` when they meet minting rules.
- Save exploratory Q&A outputs under `notes/` as `type: Note`.
- Prefer concrete terminology from sources over generic summaries.
- Always cite sources in a `# Citations` section on factual pages.
