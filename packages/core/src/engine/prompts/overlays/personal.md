## Schema pack overlay: personal

- Capture durable ideas under `topics/`; keep pages concise.
- Use `notes/` for reflections and saved query outputs (`type: Note`).
- Prefer entity-style pages (goals, habits, people, projects) with light structure.
- Journal-style sources: extract durable facts and link to entity pages; avoid verbatim diary dumps on concept pages.
- Books and literary works are valid content — organize by work, character, theme, or chapter rather than skipping for topical mismatch.
